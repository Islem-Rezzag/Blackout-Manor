# Concept Image Prompt

Create premium concept art for an original video game called **Blackout Manor**.

Show a **spectator-first top-down / 2.5D cutaway manor** during a stormy masquerade night. The image should depict **one connected gothic manor floorplan**, not isolated room cards. The camera is a slightly elevated **Sims-like cutaway overview**, showing multiple connected rooms, corridors, thresholds, and doors in one coherent house.

The house should include:
- a **Grand Hall**
- a **Dining / Meeting Room** with a long table for ten
- a **Kitchen**
- a **Library**
- a **Study**
- a **Ballroom**
- a **Greenhouse / Conservatory**
- a **Surveillance Hall**
- a **Generator Room**
- hints of a **Servants’ Corridor**

The visual mood is:
- **storm-noir manor atmosphere**
- **warm interior lamp and candle light**
- **cold blue storm light at windows and exterior edges**
- rain streaks on glass
- occasional lightning glow
- polished wood, brass, marble, velvet, glass, and damp stone

Characters:
- show **ten stylized realistic human agents** in masquerade attire
- elegant coats, dresses, masks, gloves, capes, shawls, jewelry, and distinct silhouettes
- absolutely **not Among Us visual identity**
- no bean characters, no cartoon capsules, no toy-like proportions
- readable posture differences: calm, alert, suspicious, shaken, confident, defiant

Action:
- some agents are moving through corridors and doors
- one room should imply a sabotage or blackout issue
- one group should be gathering toward the dining room as if a meeting is about to begin
- one surveillance area should hint at multi-camera observation

Style targets:
- cinematic game key art
- polished, premium, atmospheric
- readable room composition
- believable spatial movement paths
- dramatic but not cluttered
- designed for a **spectator-first social deduction game**

Negative guidance:
- no copyrighted Among Us likeness
- no flat mobile dashboard UI
- no isolated floating room boxes
- no sci-fi spaceship
- no comedic casual party aesthetic
- no chibi bean bodies

Short style reminder:
**Sims-like cutaway readability, storm-noir manor atmosphere, masquerade humans, premium spectator-first game runtime.**
